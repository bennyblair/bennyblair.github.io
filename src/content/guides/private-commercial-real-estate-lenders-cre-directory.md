## 1. Introduction to Private CRE Lending

Private commercial real estate lenders are non-bank institutions or individuals who provide finance for commercial property transactions. Unlike traditional banks, private lenders offer flexible solutions, faster approvals, and tailored products for complex or time-sensitive deals.

Private lending is especially popular for:
- Property acquisitions
- Development projects
- Refinancing
- Bridging finance
- Special situations (e.g., credit-impaired borrowers, urgent settlements)

For a full overview of private lending in Australia, see our guide: [What is Private Lending?](./what-is-private-lending-australia.md)

## 2. Why Choose Private Lenders for Commercial Real Estate?

Private lenders offer several advantages for CRE borrowers:
- **Speed:** Approvals and settlements are often much faster than banks.
- **Flexibility:** Willingness to consider unique property types, structures, and borrower profiles.
- **Customisation:** Loan terms can be tailored to suit the project or investment.
- **Less Red Tape:** Streamlined documentation and assessment processes.

Private lenders are ideal for:
- Developers needing quick access to capital
- Investors acquiring properties in competitive markets
- Businesses refinancing or restructuring debt
- Borrowers with non-standard income or credit histories

## 3. CRE Lending Criteria: What Private Lenders Look For

While private lenders are flexible, they still assess key criteria:
- **Property Type:** Office, retail, industrial, development sites, etc.
- **Location:** Metro, regional, growth corridors
- **Loan-to-Value Ratio (LVR):** Typically up to 65-75% for CRE
- **Borrower Profile:** Experience, financials, credit history
- **Exit Strategy:** How the loan will be repaid (sale, refinance, etc.)
- **Project Feasibility:** For development finance, detailed plans and costings

## 5. How to Compare and Choose a CRE Lender

When selecting a private lender for commercial real estate, consider:
- **Track Record:** Experience in CRE lending
- **Product Range:** Types of loans offered
- **Speed:** Approval and settlement timelines
- **Transparency:** Fees, rates, and terms
- **Flexibility:** Willingness to structure deals
- **Reputation:** Reviews, testimonials, industry standing

Always compare multiple lenders and seek professional advice before committing.

---

## 6. Key Risks and Considerations

- **Higher Interest Rates:** Private loans often carry higher rates than banks
- **Shorter Terms:** Many private CRE loans are short-term (6-36 months)
- **Due Diligence:** Always verify lender credentials and loan terms
- **Exit Strategy:** Ensure a clear plan for repayment
- **Regulatory Compliance:** Confirm the lender operates within Australian financial regulations

## 7. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### What is a private commercial real estate lender?
A non-bank institution or individual providing finance for commercial property transactions.

### How do private CRE loans differ from bank loans?
Private loans are typically faster, more flexible, and tailored to complex deals, but may have higher rates and shorter terms.

### What types of properties do private CRE lenders finance?
Office, retail, industrial, development sites, mixed-use, and more.

### How do I qualify for a private CRE loan?
Criteria include property type, location, LVR, borrower profile, and exit strategy.

### Are private CRE lenders regulated?
Reputable lenders operate within Australian financial regulations. Always verify credentials.
