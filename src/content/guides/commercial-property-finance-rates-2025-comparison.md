## 2. 2025 Rate Overview: Commercial Property Loans

As of late 2025, commercial property finance rates in Australia typically range from:
- **Bank Loans:** 6.25% – 8.00% p.a. (variable/fixed)
- **Non-Bank Lenders:** 7.50% – 10.50% p.a.
- **Private Lenders:** 9.00% – 14.00% p.a. (short-term, bespoke deals)

Rates vary based on loan type, borrower profile, property sector, and deal complexity. Always request a tailored quote from multiple lenders.

## 3. Key Factors Influencing Rates

Several factors shape commercial property finance rates in 2025:
- **Official Cash Rate:** RBA policy impacts base lending rates
- **Lender Type:** Banks, non-banks, and private lenders price risk differently
- **Loan-to-Value Ratio (LVR):** Higher LVRs often attract higher rates
- **Property Type:** Office, retail, industrial, development, etc.
- **Borrower Profile:** Credit history, experience, financials
- **Deal Complexity:** Bespoke or urgent deals may incur premium pricing
- **Market Conditions:** Supply/demand, sector performance, economic outlook

## 4. Rate Comparison: Major Lender Types

### Banks
- **Rates:** 6.25% – 8.00% p.a.
- **Features:** Lower rates, strict criteria, longer approval times
- **Best For:** Prime borrowers, stable income, standard properties

### Non-Bank Lenders
- **Rates:** 7.50% – 10.50% p.a.
- **Features:** Flexible criteria, faster approvals, wider product range
- **Best For:** Investors, developers, non-standard deals

### Private Lenders
- **Rates:** 9.00% – 14.00% p.a.
- **Features:** Fast, bespoke, short-term solutions
- **Best For:** Urgent settlements, credit-impaired borrowers, complex projects

## 5. Rate Trends and Predictions for 2025

- **Rising Base Rates:** RBA increases have pushed up commercial lending rates
- **Tighter Credit:** Banks remain cautious, favouring lower-risk deals
- **Growth in Non-Bank & Private Lending:** More borrowers turning to flexible lenders
- **Sector Variations:** Industrial and logistics properties attract lower rates; retail and development finance may be higher
- **Short-Term Premiums:** Bridging and bespoke loans command higher rates

Expert tip: Always compare offers and negotiate terms. Rate is important, but so are fees, flexibility, and lender reputation.

## 6. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### What is the average commercial property finance rate in Australia for 2025?
Bank rates average 6.25%–8.00%, non-banks 7.50%–10.50%, private lenders 9.00%–14.00%.

### Why do private lender rates differ from banks?
Private lenders price for speed, flexibility, and risk, resulting in higher rates for bespoke deals.

### How can I get the best rate for my commercial property loan?
Compare multiple lenders, improve your borrower profile, and negotiate terms.

### Are rates expected to rise or fall in 2025?
Rates have risen due to RBA policy, but future movements depend on economic conditions.

### What other costs should I consider?
Upfront fees, ongoing charges, exit fees, and legal costs can impact total borrowing cost.
